Who's behind ClickyKeys

ClickyKeys is a hobby project built and maintained by a single developer.

Reksaku

Developer & maintainer of ClickyKeys

ClickyKeys started as a small side project — a lightweight key & click counter that looks good as an OBS overlay. It grew into a free, open-source Windows app used by streamers, content creators and gamers who like seeing their inputs on screen.

The app is written in C# and released under the GPL-3.0 license. Everything runs locally on your machine — no accounts, no tracking, no data leaving your device. Feedback, bug reports and feature ideas are always welcome on GitHub.
